# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Super Administrators**: Manage global configuration, colleges, staff registries, and high-level performance analytics across all units.
- **Instructors / ANOs**: Manage the exam lifecycle (creation, targeted assignments, and scoring), cadet registries (bulk CSV import), and real-time violation monitoring.
- **Cadets (Students)**: View their personalized learning dashboard, take high-integrity proctored exams, download study materials, and track their performance history.

## Product Purpose
A production-grade, secure, and proctored examination portal for the National Cadet Corps (NCC) to ensure secure, high-integrity assessment workflows with real-time proctoring heuristics, targeted assignment, and robust administrative cadet management.

## Brand Personality
- **Disciplined**: Highly structured, structured grids, military-precision alignment.
- **Professional**: Understated, elegant color schemes (Navy, Gold, Stone) that align with the NCC identity.
- **Reliable**: Zero-latency interfaces, robust visual feedback, error-resilient transactions.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered consumer SaaS dashboards with heavy gradients and over-rounded cards.
- Laggy and unresponsive lists that fail or freeze when loading thousands of records.
- Poorly styled, vague form validation errors.

## Design Principles
1. **Engineering Precision**: Clean alignment, solid borders, structured table grids, and consistent spacing scales.
2. **High Integrity**: Uncompromising clarity in alerts, status badges, and validation reports.
3. **Graceful Performance**: Interfaces must remain interactive and snappy even when displaying large datasets (e.g. up to 9,000 spike users / thousands of cadet records).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast text (meeting WCAG AAA contrast ratio where possible, minimum 4.5:1).
- Screen-reader friendly forms and tables with explicit ARIA labels.
- Reduced motion optimization for all transitions and alerts.
